The Majik Bloom Story

Founded in 2021, Majik Bloom emerged from a passion for cultivating exceptional genetics. Our founder, a lifelong enthusiast with a fascination for ancient techniques, set out to create a seed stock with great flavours, helping you connect to your innate supernatural abilities.

Through years of procuring genetics, selective breeding and careful nurturing, we've developed strains that not only deliver exceptional quality but also carry the magical essence that our name promises.
