About Prizm 33

Prizm 33 is a truly magical strain that has been cultivated in secret gardens for centuries. This indica-dominant hybrid combines ancient landrace genetics with modern magical enhancements.

The buds are dense and dark purple with shimmering trichomes that seem to capture moonlight. The aroma is a complex blend of earthy forest floor, sweet berries, and a hint of mystical incense.

This strain is particularly valued for its calming and introspective effects, making it ideal for evening use, meditation, and magical practices.
