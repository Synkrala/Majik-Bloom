Returns & Refunds

Our policy on returns, exchanges, and refunds for your magical seed purchases

Returns Policy

At Majik Bloom Seeds, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your seeds for any reason, we offer a 30-day return policy.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the original packaging
  • Unopened and unused
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Opened seed packets
  • Custom or personalized orders
  • Items purchased from third-party sellers

Refund Process

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact our customer service team at returns@majikbloom.com with your order number and reason for return.

Refund Timeline

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to an error on our part (wrong item shipped, defective product, etc.).

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different strain, please contact us at exchanges@majikbloom.com. You will be responsible for any price difference and shipping costs.

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged upon arrival. If you need to exchange a defective item, please send us an email with photos of the damaged product.

Important Return Notice

Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns on opened seed packets for safety and quality control reasons. Please inspect your seeds upon arrival and contact us immediately if there are any issues.
