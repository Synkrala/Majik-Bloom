Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our magical seeds, shipping policies, and legal compliance.

Important Legal Notice

Majik Bloom Seeds sells all seeds as collectible souvenirs and for genetic preservation purposes only. It is the responsibility of the buyer to ensure compliance with local, state, and federal laws regarding hemp and cannabis seeds. We ship throughout the US in compliance with the 2018 Farm Bill, but germination may be restricted in some areas.

Product Questions

What types of seeds do you offer?

We offer a variety of high-quality, genetically stable hemp seeds with various cannabinoid profiles. Our collection includes CBD-rich, CBG-rich, and other specialty varieties, all carefully selected for their unique properties and growing characteristics.

Are your seeds feminized?

Yes, the majority of our seeds are feminized to ensure female plants, which produce the cannabinoid-rich flowers. We clearly label each product with its specific characteristics.

What is the germination rate of your seeds?

Our seeds have a germination rate of 85% or higher when stored and handled properly. We recommend proper storage conditions (cool, dark, and dry) to maintain viability.

How should I store my seeds?

For optimal preservation, store seeds in a cool, dark, and dry place. Many collectors use airtight containers with silica gel packets to maintain proper humidity levels.

Shipping & Delivery

Do you ship throughout the United States?

Yes, we ship to all 50 states in compliance with federal law. All seeds are sold as collectible souvenirs and for genetic preservation purposes only, in accordance with the 2018 Farm Bill.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. We also offer expedited shipping options at checkout for faster delivery. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

How are seeds packaged for shipping?

Seeds are carefully packaged in discreet, protective containers to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Our packaging is designed to protect the seeds from damage during transit while maintaining their privacy.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping regulations for hemp seeds vary widely, and we cannot guarantee compliance with all international laws.

Legal & Compliance

Are your seeds legal to purchase?

Yes, our seeds contain less than 0.3% THC on a dry weight basis, making them legal under the 2018 Farm Bill. However, it is the buyer's responsibility to verify local and state regulations before purchasing or germinating seeds.

What is your compliance statement?

Majik Bloom Seeds operates in full compliance with the 2018 Farm Bill. All seeds are sold as collectible souvenirs and for genetic preservation purposes only. We do not encourage or endorse the germination of seeds in jurisdictions where it may be prohibited by law.

Do you provide certificates of analysis?

Yes, we provide Certificates of Analysis (COAs) for our seed varieties upon request. These documents verify the cannabinoid profiles and compliance with federal regulations.

What is the minimum age to purchase?

You must be at least 21 years of age to purchase from Majik Bloom Seeds. By placing an order, you confirm that you meet this age requirement.

Returns & Guarantees

What is your return policy?

Due to the nature of our products, we do not accept returns on seeds. However, if you receive damaged or incorrect items, please contact us within 7 days of receipt, and we will work to resolve the issue.

Do you offer germination guarantees?

While we ensure our seeds are of the highest quality, we cannot guarantee germination rates as they are affected by storage conditions and cultivation methods after purchase. We recommend proper storage and handling to maintain viability.

What if my order doesn't arrive?

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact us with your order number. We'll track your package and work with you to resolve any shipping issues.
